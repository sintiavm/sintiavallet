Target Audience

Here we will analyze who this site is suitable for.

Who

Anyone who is interested in what is happening in the sky above his head or somewhere in the world

Age

From 18 to 70 years. Maybe more, but I only heard about such people.

Education

A person must have computer or phone skills to type a page in a browser.

Motivation

Desire not to get wet in the rain or not look silly in the eyes of other people.

Personas

Persona 1:

This is Raul. He is 27 years old, single, no children. He likes to walk outdoors in good company, that is, with me. Aydar does not like to be ill. Since it is still quite cold in our area even in summer and often rains, he often has to watch the weather forecast so as not to be taken by surprise. Such a site is very useful to him.

Persona 2:

This is Gerardo. 34 years old, married, 2 child. A very cheerful person, he likes to have fun. Often travels to work in other cities. He does not have a car, so he must rely on public modes of transport. Knowing the weather in such cases is extremely important to determine what things to take on a business trip, whether to dress warmly, whether to bring an umbrella or sunscreen with you.
